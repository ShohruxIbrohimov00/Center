Circle: The number of degrees of arc in a circle is 360. The number of radians of arc in a circle is $2\pi$. The area of a circle is $A = \pi r^2$ and the circumference is $C = 2\pi r$.

Rectangle: The area of a rectangle is $A = lw$.

Triangle: The sum of the measures in degrees of the angles of a triangle is 180. The area of a triangle is $A = \frac{1}{2}bh$.

Pythagorean Theorem: $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$

Special Right Triangles:

30-60-90 triangle: The side lengths are in the ratio $1, \sqrt{3}, 2$.

45-45-90 triangle: The side lengths are in the ratio $1, 1, \sqrt{2}$.


Volume Formulas
